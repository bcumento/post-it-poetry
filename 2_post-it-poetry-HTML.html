About

This National Poetry Month, bring communal spaces alive through poetry. Celebrate the themes of Passover and Spring by reclaiming walls, hallways, and elevators, and filling them with post-its full of the words that move us during the season of renewal.

Use the hashtag #PostItPoetry to share your own post-its and post-it installations with the global online community. Everyone has a message of hope, what’s yours?
